Project Plan: CallaoBank

Site Name

CallaoBank

A bank designed specifically for the residents and businesses of Callao, Perú, providing accessible financial services with local understanding.

Site Purpose

The CallaoBank website serves as a digital gateway to accessible banking for the Callao community, providing:

  • Information about banking services (savings accounts, loans, investments)
  • Online account registration and application
  • Connection with local banking professionals
  • Financial education resources
  • Support for small business growth

Scenarios

1. New Customer

"I've never had a bank account before. How do I create an account and what are the benefits?"

  • Open Your Account
  • Benefits of banking with CallaoBank
  • Where are we located

2. Business Owner

"I run a small business in Callao and need funding. What loan options does CallaoBank offer?"

  • Business loan product descriptions
  • Services provided
  • Loan Processor Assignment
